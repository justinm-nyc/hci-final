Expert's Circle

Purpose

The purpose of the Expert's Circle is to allow users to send their mood logs and data to a panel of professionals. Once the mood logs and data are received by the professionals, they will then evaluate the user's well being and offer advice if needed. Users are encouraged to only send their information if they are comfortable with their moods and data being read and analyzed.
